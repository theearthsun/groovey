THE CLEANING PROCESS

Remove surface dust / grim using a clean carbon fibre brush.

Literally apply the PULSATIN PRE CLEAN to the side of the record to be cleaned. Try to cover the whole half record but avoid the centre label.

Place the record on a clean lint free towel / Mat and rub the required PULSATIN surfactant at 12, 3, 6, and 9 o'clock. Follow the grooves in full circles with gentle force.

Using a clean goats hair brush, brush into the grooves. Follow the groove in both directions 8 times. Pay special attention to any fingerprinted areas, leave to sink into grooves for 8 minutes before moving to other side.

Complete both sides by repeating the steps.

Rinse off well with fresh distilled water.

Repeat steps (2, 3 and 4).

Leave for 8 minutes before moving record to next stage!

Rinse clean with distilled water. Allow to fully dry.

PREPARING THE SURFACTANT

1) Fill a litre of distilled water in a sealable plastic/glass bottle.

2) In a (COVER MOUTH) well ventilated room open the required PULSATIN SURFACTANT and quickly drop the bottle into the distilled water bottle and seal.

3) Allow 8 minutes for the mixture to mix.

4) Use directly as described in the cleaning process.
