THE CLEANING PROCESS

  1. Remove surface dust / grim using a clean carbon fibre brush.
  2. Literally apply the PULSATIN PRE CLEAN to the side of record to be cleaned. Try to cover the whole of half record but avoid the centre label.
  3. Place the record on a clean lint free Towel / Mat and rub the PULSATIN PRE CLEAN SOULTION at 12, 3, 6 and 9 o'clock. Follow the grooves in full circles with gentle force.
  4. Using a clean goats hair brush, brush the "PULSATIN PRE CLEAN SOULTION" into the grooves. Follow the groove in both directions 8 times. Pay special attention to any finger printed areas, leave to sink into grooves for 8 minutes before moving to other side.
  5. Complete both sides by repeating the steps.
  6. Rinse off well with fresh distilled water.
  7. Repeat steps (2, 3 and 4) but swap the pre clean solution with the "PULSATIN VINYL SURFACTANT MIXER" BB, AB, PP OR AP.
  8. Leave for 8 minutes before moving record to next stage!

Rinse clean with distilled water. Allow to dry completely then place records in brand new clean record sleeves.
