FOUR TOOLS FOR FOUR JOBS

(1) BB (Bass Boost) – Highlights lower sound waves.

(2) AB (Acoustic Blend) – Midrange: Guitars and Piano.

(3) PP (Perception) – Higher waves: Vocals and Cymbals.

(4) AP (Audio Pure) – The full sound stage experience.

THE CLEANING PROCESS

  1. Surface dust removal with carbon fibre brush.
  2. Apply PULSATIN PRE CLEAN (avoid label).
  3. Rub at 12, 3, 6, and 9 o'clock with lint-free towel.
  4. Brush with goats hair brush 8 times both ways. Sink for 8 mins.
  5. Repeat for both sides.
  6. Rinse with distilled water.
  7. Repeat steps 2-4 using the MIXER (BB, AB, PP, or AP).
  8. Leave for 8 minutes before final rinse.
